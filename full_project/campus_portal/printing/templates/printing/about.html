About Campus Portal

A unified digital platform built to make campus life easier — connecting students to essential services without the queues.

🖨️

Repro Services

Upload documents, set your print preferences — color, paper size, binding — and skip the long queue at the reprography centre.

🛒

College Store

Browse stationery, books, calculators and more. Add to cart, pay online, and collect at your convenience using your order ID.

🔐

Secure & Role-Based

All accounts are created and managed by the Super Admin. Separate dashboards for Repro Admins and Store Admins keep operations clean.

📌 How It Works

1️⃣
Login
Use the credentials provided by the Super Admin to sign in.
2️⃣
Choose a Service
Head to Repro to submit a print job, or browse the Store for stationery.
3️⃣
Pay & Order
Complete a quick demo payment to confirm your order. Your order ID is generated instantly.
4️⃣
Collect
Walk up to the counter, show your order ID, and collect — no waiting in line.
📬
Questions or Issues?
Contact your campus IT admin or the front desk for account-related help.
