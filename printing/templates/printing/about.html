Mini Project · Campus Services

Campus Portal

A digital gateway to campus services — skip the queues, place your orders online, and pick up when ready.

🎓

What is Campus Portal?

Campus Portal is a mini-project website built to streamline two core campus services — the College Reprography Centre and the College Store. Students no longer need to stand in physical queues or wait around to place an order; everything can be booked online in advance. Users still come in physically to collect their orders, but by then the order is already taken, confirmed, and ready — saving significant time and effort for everyone involved.

🖨️

Reprography (Repro) Service

Submit documents for printing directly from your device — no USB drives, no in-person queuing.

📄 All document types PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, images and more
🎨 Color options Black & White (₹2/page) or Color (₹10/page)
📐 Paper & binding A4 / A3, single / double sided, spiral, thermal or hardcover
🔖 Order tracking Watch your order move from Pending → Printing → Done
🛒

College Store

Browse and order campus essentials — stationery, books, lab supplies and more — without waiting in line at the counter.

  • Browse products with category filters and search
  • Add items to cart, review, and checkout instantly
  • Track your order status (Pending → Ready for Pickup → Completed)
  • Collect in person once you get the "Ready for Pickup" update
⚙️

How It Works

The workflow is simple by design:

  • Students log in, place their repro or store orders online
  • Repro/Store admins see incoming orders on their dashboards, process them, and update status
  • Students come in physically only to collect — no queuing, no waiting to place the order
  • A super admin manages users, roles, and has visibility over all orders
💡

Project Note

This is a mini project developed as part of an academic exercise. It demonstrates a full-stack Django web application with role-based access control, file upload & processing, cart & order management, and a clean, responsive UI. Physical collection is still required — the portal bridges the gap between placing and processing orders, making campus services faster and more organised for everyone.
